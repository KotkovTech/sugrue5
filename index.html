What We Do

Our Capabilities

Comprehensive solutions for wind farm development, from initial site preparation to ongoing maintenance and everything in between.

Site Access Roads & Hardstands

Expert construction of site access roads and crane hardstands capable of withstanding 500T to 1,000T cranes in all conditions.

  • Floating roads in deep bogs
  • Mountain range access roads
  • Hard rock cut/fill hardstands
  • Abnormal load accessibility

Cable Trenching (Ducting)

Experienced cable trenching in difficult ground conditions, crossing waterways and roads with the highest quality standards.

  • Difficult ground conditions
  • Waterway and road crossings
  • High quality trenching standards
  • Efficient power delivery

Roadway Maintenance

Comprehensive maintenance contracts on wind farms across Ireland, from general maintenance to complete road reconstruction.

  • Road resurfacing & re-grading
  • Drainage repairs & maintenance
  • Environmental protection controls
  • Complete road reconstruction
